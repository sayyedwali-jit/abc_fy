About the Institute

Jamia Institute of Technology is a premier institution dedicated to excellence in technical education. Our Computer Engineering department is at the forefront of innovation, nurturing students to become skilled professionals and leaders in the tech industry.

Computer Engineering Department

Our department boasts state-of-the-art laboratories, experienced faculty, and a curriculum designed to meet the evolving demands of the technology sector. We focus on holistic development, encouraging students to participate in coding competitions, hackathons, and research projects.
